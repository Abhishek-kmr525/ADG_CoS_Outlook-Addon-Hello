 POLICY UPDATE
SAP ERP Payroll Update — Effective March 1, 2026 — Transit, Water & Field Operations Staff

Dear Employee,

The City of Saskatoon HR Division has updated the Overtime and Shift Differential Policy for all field-based employees in Transit, Water Utilities, and Field Operations, effective March 1, 2026. This update has required changes to SAP ERP payroll wage codes and the SAP Employee Self-Service (ESS) portal.

All eligible employees must re-verify their direct deposit preferences and payroll profile in SAP ESS to ensure overtime pay and shift differentials are calculated correctly under the new wage codes. This is a mandatory step that cannot be completed by supervisors or payroll staff on your behalf.

SAP Payroll Changes — What's Updating March 1

✔  New overtime multiplier rates — SAP wage type codes updated
✔  Shift differential codes reconfigured in SAP ERP
✔  Direct deposit account verification required for all new SAP pay codes
✔  Union employees must re-acknowledge updated pay terms via SAP ESS

Employees who do not complete SAP ESS verification by February 28 may experience a delay in overtime and shift differential payments beginning with the March 15 pay cycle. Sign in using your City Entra ID credentials — same as Outlook and Teams — and navigate to My Profile → Banking & Payment to confirm.
