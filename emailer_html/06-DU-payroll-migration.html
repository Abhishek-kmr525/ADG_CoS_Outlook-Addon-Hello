SAP ERP Payroll System Upgrade — Employee Self-Service Verification Required

Dear Employee,

The City of Saskatoon is completing a major upgrade to our SAP ERP payroll and HR platform. As part of the data migration to the new SAP environment, all employees must verify and re-confirm their personal and direct deposit banking information in the SAP Employee Self-Service (ESS) portal before the system cutover.

Employees who do not complete verification by the deadline will experience a delay in direct deposit for the March 15 pay cycle. The City is unable to process payroll for unverified profiles in the new SAP environment.

SAP ESS Verification — Deadline & Requirements
Deadline: February 28, 2026 — 5:00 PM CST
Portal: SAP Employee Self-Service (ESS) — Entra ID sign-in required
What to confirm: Legal name, employee ID, banking & direct deposit details
Affected pay cycle: March 15, 2026 — first payroll on new SAP environment

Click below to access the SAP ESS portal. Sign in with your City of Saskatoon Entra ID credentials — the same username and password you use for Outlook and Teams. Once logged in, navigate to My Profile → Banking & Payment and confirm or update your details.
