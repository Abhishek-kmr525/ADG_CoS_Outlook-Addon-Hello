 POLICY UPDATE
Flexible Work Arrangement Policy (FWA-2026-01) — Effective March 1, 2026

Dear Employee,

The City of Saskatoon has updated its Flexible Work Arrangement Policy (FWA-2026-01) effective March 1, 2026. All employees with active hybrid or remote work arrangements must re-register their home office setup in the City's updated Employee Workspace Registry, now integrated with SAP ERP HR module. This ensures our records are aligned for payroll jurisdiction, OHS compliance, and network security monitoring under our updated Entra ID Conditional Access policies for remote endpoints.

Required Registration Information — SAP HR Module

✔  Confirmed home office address (for SAP payroll tax jurisdiction)
✔  City-issued equipment serial numbers (Intune/SCCM enrollment verification)
✔  Internet service provider details (for Entra ID Conditional Access compliance)
✔  Emergency contact at remote work location
✔  Supervisor acknowledgement (auto-routed via SAP workflow)

Employees who do not complete registration by February 28 may have their remote work arrangement suspended and their Entra ID Conditional Access policy reverted to on-premises-only access. Sign in with your City credentials via the button below.

Register My Home Office — SAP ESS Portal →

Questions? Contact HR at HRInfo@saskatoon.ca or log an ITSM request for IT-related registration issues.
